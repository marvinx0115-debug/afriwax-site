Our Story

Crafted with Heritage, Made for You

AfriWax was born from a love of African textile artistry. We connect the ancient craft of wax print — a technique perfected over centuries in West Africa — with the modern desire for personal expression and custom fit.

Every garment we produce honors the culture, the craft, and the wearer. We work directly with licensed Realwax suppliers and a network of skilled African tailors to bring you authentic quality, at accessible prices.
