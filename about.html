Our Story

About AfriWax

Crafted with heritage. Made for you. Worn with pride.

Where Tradition Meets Personal Expression

AfriWax was born from a deep love of African textile artistry and a belief that everyone deserves to wear garments made with authenticity, skill, and personal meaning.

We connect the ancient craft of wax print — a technique perfected over centuries in West Africa — with the modern desire for personal expression and custom fit. Every garment we produce honors the culture, the craft, and the wearer.

We work directly with licensed Realwax suppliers and a network of skilled African tailors who have spent decades perfecting their art. This isn't factory production — it's generations of skill applied to your specific vision.
